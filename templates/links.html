Inertia was founded in Southern California in early 2002 with Vincent Fernandez, John Oden and Arthur Rodriguez. Playing with reckless abandon and with a zeal for attention, Inertia quickly became the local mosh-able must-see metal group around, playing original bone crusing tunes such as 'Dun Dun Dun Dun' and 'Where Were You?'.

Inertia is currently in the studio working on their long awaited full length record.
